Phishing Detection Introduction

What is Phishing?

  • Phishing is a cyber attack used to steal sensitive information.
  • Attackers impersonate legitimate organizations or individuals.
  • Phishing attempts can occur via email, websites, or messages.
  • It aims to obtain passwords, credit card numbers, or personal data.
  • Phishing messages often appear legitimate but are deceptive.
